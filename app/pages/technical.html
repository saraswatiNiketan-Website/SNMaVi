Computer Engineering




Introduction to Computer Engineering

In our school, technical course is one of the facility that is advantage for the students. They learn different subjects like Web designing, Electronics devices and circuit, Computer network and along with these they also learn different programming languages as well. Each subject have their own scope for the bright future of the students. This course aims to produce professionally able manpower in field of computers. Actually, the duration of this course is five years with four years theoretical classes and one year practical but in order to reduce the duration the course is arrange in such a way that it is completed with the completion of grade 12. This allows the students to study further without wasting their precious time.

Technical Computer Engineering course include:

1 year theoretical class in grade 9
1 year theoretical class in grade 10
3 months basic level OJT on related domain
(During the break after giving SEE)
1 year theoretical class in grade 11
3 months intermediate level OJT on related domain
(During the break after examination)
1 year theoretical class in grade 12
6 months advance level OJT on related domain
(During the break after examination)
